TouchSounds Support

Use TouchSounds, let hearing-impaired friends hear the sounds of the world and speak out for the world.

The App supports recognition of more than 100 languages and converts them into text in real time.

It has built-in a variety of natural sounds and converts them into the delicate vibration of mobile phone motors, making the sounds touchable. Good news for ASMR lovers.
